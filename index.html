About Panchakarma & Philosophy:

Dr. Wagh follows the classical Panchakarma protocols of Vamana (emesis therapy), Virechana (purgation), Basti (enema therapies), Nasya (nasal administration), and Raktamokshana (blood purification). Each treatment is customized after thorough dosha assessment and examination of physiology, mind-body constitution, and lifestyle habits.

Her approach integrates ancient Ayurvedic wisdom with modern diagnostic techniques to ensure safe, effective, and sustainable healing outcomes. Patient education on diet, daily routine, and seasonal care is an integral part of her practice.
