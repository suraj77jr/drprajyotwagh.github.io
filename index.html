Our Vision

"Adopt The Ayurveda" is a small effort with a big vision of making Ayurvedic Treatments (both preventive & curative) approachable & affordable to the whole community. This is possible with special thrust on patient education to adopt lifestyle & diet according to body constitution & the disease they are suffering with.

Dr. Praveen Rustagi is committed to providing compassionate care believing that every human being should have access to quality health care. It takes healthy parents & healthy neighborhoods to raise a healthy child.
